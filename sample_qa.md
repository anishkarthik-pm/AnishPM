## Citation Standards

Every answer includes:
1. **Clear fact statement** (1-2 sentences max)
2. **Official source link** from HDFC/SEBI/AMFI
3. **"Last updated from official sources"** timestamp note
4. **No speculation** or predictions

## Refusal Standards

All refusals:
1. **Politely decline** investment advice
2. **Explain why** (e.g., cannot predict returns)
3. **Offer alternatives** (links to educational resources)
4. **Suggest factual questions** the assistant CAN answer
5. **Maintain helpful tone** without being preachy
